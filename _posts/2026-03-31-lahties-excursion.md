On March 30th, we headed out for what has become a yearly classic: our excursion to Helsinki. What might look like just another trip quickly proves to be something much more. Beyond the bus ride and good company, it’s a day filled with conversations, ideas, and connections that can genuinely shape what comes next.

This year, we had the chance to hear from people deeply involved in the Helsinki startup ecosystem. Viivi Marttinen from Herizon, Jussi Kallasvuo from Antler, Aleksei Filonov from Aalto Entrepreneurship Society, and Thai Tran from Aalto Founder School shared their perspectives. Together, they gave us an honest, behind-the-scenes look at how things actually work, from building early-stage ideas to navigating the broader startup landscape.

![](/assets/images/dsc01037.jpg)

What stood out most wasn’t just the information, but the feeling. You could sense ideas forming, perspectives shifting, and motivation growing stronger throughout the day. It’s that kind of environment where inspiration doesn’t feel forced, it just happens naturally, often somewhere between a talk, a question, or a casual conversation.

By the time we headed back, it was clear that the day had delivered more than just a schedule of visits. New connections were made, thoughts were sparked, and for many, the next steps felt a little clearer.

We’re genuinely grateful to everyone who joined and made the day what it was.\
