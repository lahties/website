#### **Most ideas don't start as "projects."**

They start as a note. A random thought. Something you almost forget about five minutes later, but somehow it keeps coming back.

"Someone should do this"\
"This could actually work."\
"What if..."

And then it just stays there. In your head. Or in your notes app. Untouched.

This is where things usually stop.\
Not because the idea isn't good.\
But because it doesn't feel ready. Not clear enough. Not detailed enough. Not serious enough.

So it waits.

This form is not about being ready.\
The ***Project Application Form*** is simply a way to take that idea one step further.\
Not to prove it. Not to perfect it.\
Just to move it.

Whether you want to build something, organise something, test something, or just see if your idea makes sense outside your own head - this is where it starts!

**What actually happens after you apply?**

1. You get structure, so the idea doesn't stay "just an idea"
2. You get people. Others who might want to build it with you
3. You get support from people who've been through the same messy starting point

It's still your idea. You're still figuring it out.\
But you're not doing it alone anymore

**So why do it?**

Because ideas don't become anything by staying where they are. And starting small is still starting.\
If something has been sitting in your head for a while, this is probably the easiest way to give it a chance!
