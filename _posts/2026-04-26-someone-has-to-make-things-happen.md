The one who keeps track of timelines.\
the one who notices when something's off.\
The one who makes sure ideas don't just stay ideas.\

**Who is this for?**

Not everything will go perfectly.

But if that excites you more than it scares you, you'll probably enjoy this.

**Why take it on?**

Because this is where you stop just observing things happen and start being the reason they do!
